## 1. The Handshake Protocol
VEND provides a physical visit verification system (The "Handshake"). 
- A Handshake is completed when a Customer enters a unique 4-digit code provided by a Vendor.
- By completing a Handshake, both parties verify their physical proximity and the satisfactory initiation or completion of a local service.
- VEND is a discovery platform and is not liable for the conduct, safety, or quality of services provided during these physical interactions.

## 2. VEND Points Economy
- Points earned within the app are "Visibility Credits" and "Discovery Rewards."
- Points have no direct cash value and cannot be withdrawn as fiat currency.
- Points can be exchanged for discounts at participating vendors. Once a discount is redeemed via point deduction, the transaction is final.

## 3. Vendor Responsibilities

- Vendors are responsible for the accuracy of their pinned locations and business hours.
