# VEND: Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Your privacy is paramount. VEND is built on "Privacy by Design" to ensure your location and personal data are protected.

## 1. Location Data Transparency
VEND is a hyperlocal discovery tool. We use your GPS location to:
- Show you vendors in your immediate vicinity.
- Provide "Proximity Reminders" when you arrive at a destination.
- Generate aggregate discovery heatmaps for your locality.

## 2. Coordinate Masking
For Home-Based Vendors:
- Exact GPS coordinates are never shown to general users.
- We display a 300m "Fuzzy Radius" circle to protect physical residential privacy.
- Exact addresses are only revealed to a Customer once a Trip is active and a Handshake is intended.

## 3. Data Redaction (PII Scrubbing)
VEND automatically scrubs Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from technical error logs. This ensures that your phone number, email, and security tokens are never stored in plain text within our monitoring systems.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell your data. Location data is shared only with the specific Vendor you are navigating to, for the purpose of safety and visit verification.
